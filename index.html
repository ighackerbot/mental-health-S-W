Finding Light in Darkness

By Michael R.

"I never thought I'd find my way through depression..."

For years, I struggled with depression in silence. Every day felt like walking through fog, unable to see a way forward. But through therapy, support groups, and the understanding of those around me, I gradually found my path to recovery.

Today, I'm not just surviving - I'm thriving. I've learned that seeking help isn't a sign of weakness, but of courage. My journey has taught me that hope exists even in the darkest moments.
