Ira Khan Opens Up on Battling Depression, Aamir Khan, Mental Health Journey & Sexual Ab*se. In this deeply emotional and inspiring podcast, Ira Khan shares her journey of battling depression and navigating through life's toughest challenges. From discussing her relationship with her father, Aamir Khan, to opening up about mental health, childhood struggles, and the impact of sexual abuse, Ira sheds light on the importance of seeking help and breaking the stigma around mental health. Her candid revelations and heartfelt story will leave you inspired to prioritize mental well-being and support others in their journey. Don’t miss this powerful conversation!
